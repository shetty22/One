VM Homes Customer Testimonials

We (wife and I) decided, out of the blue, that Dec was the was right time to buy a house, after having lived in the country for not more than 18 months and with not much of a credit history. Enter scene Vidya and Michelle. Vidya was an acquaintance before we decided to buy the house. Little did we know they both would end up as good buddies by the end of our home buying process. Until we met them, my idea of realtors was “this is an easy job, shouldn’t be too hard to find and show homes to prospective buyer/sellers". Boy oh Boy, Was a mistaken!! Vidya and Michelle walked us through the complete process and were extremely honest and transparent about the process. That's when I was made aware of the kind of effort, time and energy that goes in this job. Being first time home buyers, we were filled with paranoia and had a lot of questions. They both patiently answered all our questions, putting us at ease, without which I probably wouldn't have been able to sleep. If they didn’t know something they were frank about it, and always got back to us with the right information. They also connected us with the mortgage bankers and worked seamlessly with the title company. Of course, like all big events in our lives, everything doesn't happen as per plan but these 2 ladies made sure that the issues were resolved and we were back on track. Long story short - Team of Vidya and Michelle are transparent, honest, good negotiators and above all they are empathetic which i think is an absolute requirement especially when dealing with first time home buyers.

Malik and Soni
Foster City
